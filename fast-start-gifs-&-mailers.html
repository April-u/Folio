Fast Start GIFs and Mailers

Comprehend Client Project | 11 November 2020

Project Details

For my second project with IBM, I was tapped to support the Fast Start Team to create 6 mailer designs and digital and print certificates. We were able to deliver a total of 13 mailers, all of which were required to be Carbon compliant, compatible with Lotus Notes, and accessible for screen readers. THE STORY

IBM Global Event, November 11, 2020

Challenge

Since single image ecards and hotspots do not meet IBM Accessibility requirements, the project required the use of html tables. This project was also my first experience with Lotus Notes, which has compatibility issues with other email providers like gmail and outlook. It also has incompatibility issues between its desktop and online apps, Lotus Notes and Verse.

As the nature of the project is event hosting, the content for the mailers came at the last minute, compressing our deadlines.

Role

My background in designing and publishing email campaigns using tools like Outlook (manual designing) and Mailchimp (html-based tool), in addition to the design and Lotus Notes guidance from our client, helped me create a table-based and html compatible, Carbon-compliant newsletter. I reached out to the client directly to request for their design brand book and provided initial content drafts and suggested content and revisions. I was able to create placeholder content which the Clients were able to work with and helped them visualize their final content.

Business Impact

From the 6 mailers requested, we were able to deliver 13 successful newsletters. Although the Fast Start event experienced a site outage, Comprehend was given excellent reviews for our newsletter contribution. They were impressed by the execution of Carbon-compliant, accessible and html table-based designs that did not require the use of the IBM external HTML-based tool, Acoustic. We saved Comprehend money by making the newsletter from scratch and buffer the impact of the event site crash.
