Let's Create

Activity | 5 Mar 2022

Project Details

As an activity, we were given a project brief to create a teaser video encouraging employees to participate in the launch of the our company's Growth Framework.

First, I researched on the topic. I tried to find a link between the new training packages and found the launch of our company rebrand, "Let's Create". Basing the content from the campaign message, I wrote the script, made the storyboards, recorded and edited the audio, looked for video pegs similar to our company branding and then proceeded to source out the background music and edit the videos.
