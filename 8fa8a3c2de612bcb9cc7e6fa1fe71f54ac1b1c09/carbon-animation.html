UI/UX Enablement Presentation | 9 July 2021

Project Details

I was asked by our Tech Lead to host Enablement session. I had a week to prepare and I could choose any topic to share. Since a fellow UI/UX designer had asked me a week before about After Effects, I decided to choose the topic on Animating for our Company Brand Guidelines. The featured video is for my presentation, where I recreated our Company's version of the animation principles. View the session recording here.




The Story

UI/UX Enablment Session, 9 July 2021

Challenge

My Tech Lead reached out to me to lead the week's enablement session within 4 day's time (July 5) and to pick any topic I'd like.

Role

A month before that, a fellow VD had asked me how to edit videos in After Effects after seeing our Product video portfolio showcase. From that request, I thought that the team might be interested on the topic of animating for our Company Branding guidelines.

Business Impact

The enablement session was attended by 6 members of our UI/UX team. I also provided an AE Carbon Animation template, and resources and steps for the animation process.
