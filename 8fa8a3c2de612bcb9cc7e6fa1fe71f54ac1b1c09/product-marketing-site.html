The Story

Product Marketing, 7 April 2021

Challenge

Due to Comprehend's leadership transition, the restructuring of our Product offerings and pricing packages meant that the content we were to work with was still undergoing intensive management review.

This project is my first introduction to our company internal marketing CMS platform.

Role

Because I had no content to work with, I took the initiative to gather and research all the documents we had on our Product and tried to create a content structure based on the data we had at the time. My LXD parter and I set up a working session in order to have easier feedback and coordination.

After the LXD was satisfied with my design, I scheduled a meeting with our Senior Designer to get clearance that our design is aligned with our Product branding. He gave favorable feedback and was overall happy with the design, giving only minor revisions. Then we had Project Managers review the approved designs.

Business Impact

With the placeholder content for the lay-out we provided, we were able to suggest which content will be needed on the Product Marketing page, and pointed out the gaps in our marketing or sales materials. I was also able to implement the process of scheduling a consultation with our Senior Designer to ensure our project is aligned with our Product style guide. This set a precedent for me to keep setting up meetings with our Senior Designer to ensure compliance to the Product brand and get regular feedback and coaching from my Senior.
