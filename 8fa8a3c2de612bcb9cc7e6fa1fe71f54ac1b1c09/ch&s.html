The Story

Product Client Project, 16 November 2021

Challenge

The project had a short development turnaround and must use Moodle as it's core structure. I had only 3 days to complete the mockup and hand it over to the front-end developer.

Role

I had limited time to work on the initial mockups but I took the initiative to add in pages that were not included on the request but necessary for the UI/UX path just in case the Client expected the design.

The most fun we had on the project was the chance to collaborate with the front-end developer and moodle consultant so that we could execute the design using Label activities and work-arounds.

Business Impact

We were able to stick to the core Company Branding despite the limitations imposed by the Moodle Bootstrap lay-out. Our asset owner commended the effort and commented that she could didn't recognize Moodle in the design anymore. In short, we were able to leverage on Moodle's tracking capabilities while customizing the design with minimal development work through work-arounds to fit our branding.
