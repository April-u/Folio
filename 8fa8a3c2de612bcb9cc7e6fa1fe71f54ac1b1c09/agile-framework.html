Agile Framework

Activity | 18 Apr 2022

Project Details

When our activity for the Framework Animation was extended to four weeks, we were given an additional video task with similar requirements to create a partner video on the Agile Framework. Since the first video covered a broader and complex topic, I focused on finishing the first video so I could recycle the parts of the video covering the Agile Framework.

In total, this video activity took 8 hours to complete, including the script, storyboard, audio sourcing and video editing where I revised some of the visual elements in the Framework Animation video that did not fit the Agile framework.
