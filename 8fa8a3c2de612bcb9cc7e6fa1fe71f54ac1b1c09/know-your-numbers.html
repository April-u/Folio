Product Client Project | 23 Mar 2022

Project Details

I was tapped by a Project Manager to work on a new client project that involved a website and video request. I was given 2 weeks to work on the video and the mockup. I requested that my partner LXD be included in the project due to the tight deadline to help me by starting work on the site wireframes while I worked on the video. The client provided the final script accompanied with a recorded audio and an initial draft of the storyboard. I was given free reign to reinterpret the storyboard into our brand design and add visual elements to accompany the subject. See the video on your left and the website mockup below.




The Story

Product Client Project | 23 Mar 2022

Challenge

The Client specifically requested for colorful palettes, light themes, positive and diverse/inclusive imagery, and creativity. However, the project had a short development turnaround and must still comply with our Company Branding and use Moodle as it's core structure. I had only 2 weeks to work on the 4.5-minute long video itself. I was given additional time to also work on the site mockup and met with the client to discuss further change requests.

Role

I had limited time to work on the videos and since the storyboard the Client provided only suggested visual ideas in a few slides, it was not enough to cover the 4.5 minute long audio script they provided. I took the initiative to re-design the storyboards with our company brand design and added more informative image cues and elements to keep it visually stimulating.

Since the Client and PM wanted a creative and colorful site and since the Moodle LMS is a little limited, I consulted with our Front-end Developer who informed me of a plugin our Product Team has developed to override the Moodle front-end constraints. I was able to get approval to use the plugin for the project from our Squad Leader and we were able to see the creative lay-outs into development.

Since the time was limited, I was also able to request for another designer, my partner LXD to be onboarded on the project and work on the wireframes for the site to have more time for the video production.

Business Impact

The Squad Leader reported that the Client was absolutely 'blown away' by the Know your Numbers video. He said he expected something average and got a "world class" product (to use the words the customer used). The Client returned for another video project, the MSK Campaign. On top of this, we were able to create the colorful, light and creative mockups the Client wanted while sticking to the Company Design System by working around the limitations imposed by the Moodle Bootstrap theme on the lay-out and re-using existing solutions/plugins our developers had developed.
