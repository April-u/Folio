Mailer Enablement Activity

LSDev UI/UX Enablement Sessions | 12 March 2021

Project Details

My LSDev UI/UX Tech Lead, Janeil Vino asked me what I would like to practice for our next enablement session and I asked if I could try out html mailers after my experience with the IBM Fast Start project earlier that year. In addition to this, I was also working on an IBM Marketing Project and I wanted to design a mailer to explain Comprehend to our Clients.

I also created the illustration on the banner because I wanted to practice IBM Design Language Illustration Guidelines, which is not a type of project that comes in Comprehend.

Embedded here is an html design I created after researching for email HTML formatting best practices on the internet. Unfortunately, importing files onto the Notes desktop app delivered compatibility issues, but this experience helped inform me of how to execute our HTML notification email for an actual external Client project with British Petroleum (bp).
