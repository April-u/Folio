The Story

Comprehend Client Project, 4 May 2021

Challenge

Our project manager was pitching Comprehend's basic offering to an internation e-learning provider based in the UK, Pearson UK. The basic offering had little leeway for front-end development.

The project manager requested for 7 pages within the day, providing me a link to our basic Comprehend site and the Client website.

Role

I was able to deliver the pages on time, and went the extra mile to use the Client branding, add content placeholders, and make a full mockup instead of te requested simple hotspots on InVision.

Business Impact

The Client was sold and the initial page drafts were used for the final web design without any revisions, saving time for production.
