The Story

Comprehend Client Project, 16 September 2021

Challenge

Due to delayed Client sign-off, I only had a week to create the actual mockup, with a daily internal and Client review for revisions.

The Client requested for an HTML email notification for quiz results. My Lotus Notes was not installed in my PC replacement and our Developer did not have the time to help us out. We were also not given any content to work with.

Role

Despite being allotted a week to create the mockup, the PM and the Client set a meeting only 3 days after to review our progress; then the Client met with the PMs for daily reviews and revisions.

Due to my Lotus Notes installation problems, I was not able to create a proper HTML table based ecard from scratch. Instead, I did a workaround by reusing the IBM Recognition mailer template and editing it with the bp rebrand. Although I was given no content to work with, I designed the template with placeholder text which the Client used for themselves. Then I coordinated with our back-end developer who was inspired to produce an email notification plugin.

Our Product Tester, Marwa Abdelgawad, also noticed our bp site had a slow loading time and wanted to make sure that our instance could handle the video request of our Client. She sent over a 40-minute video for our developer to work with. Since the Client specified that their video would only last for 2 minutes, I stepped in and created an optimized 2-minute video with audio for our developer to use.

Business Impact

Created the first implementation of the Comprehend Standard Design template based on the IBM Wellbeing site. Our Comprehend Design Lead, Adrian Fortuna, was very pleased with the results and celebrated the achievement on the Comprehend Team Updates. David Hoffman, the Comprehend Program and Assets Manager was also excited by the design and the what it meant for future project design implementation. Our PM also mentioned that the Client was very happy and had little complaints, thanks to the design.
