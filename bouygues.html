Bouygues

Comprehend Client Project | 11 November 2020

Project Details

For my first external client project for IBM, I was tapped by Tin Tecson for a French Client, Bouygues Telecom. I was assigned to a Project Manager, Lindsey Markell, who was aware that we were new and offered to brief us on the Comprehend Catalog Plugin which will be the basis of our project.

She also supplied us with the brand site of our Client and included us in key meetings with the Client to understand the Client's vision for the site purpose, user goals and journey. She also practiced transparency for the project, sharing with us previous Comprehend projects to show us what could be achieved and always documenting our meetings and timelines and checklists on an excel sheet which I have not seen other PMs do. The Story

Comprehend Client Project, 11 November 2020

Challenge

Together with my partner, Lara Camacho, this would be our first project with a Client and we had no background or access to moodle at the time on top of not having access to our developer from Comprehend, Reggie Nagui.

Role

I had limited time to work on the initial mockups. When I had some down time during the Holiday break, I completely redesigned the mockups to align with our external Client, which pleased our Project Manager at the time, Lindsey Markell.

Lara and I also took initiative to add in pages that were not included on the request but necessary for the UI/UX path just in case the Client expected the design.

Business Impact

The project met with some difficulty due to lack of developer consultations. However, we were able to deliver what was required of us and the Project Managers were able to sort out the issues that arose from development.
