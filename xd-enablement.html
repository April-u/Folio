XD Enablement Activity

LSDev UI/UX Enablement Sessions | 18 February 2021

Project Details

For my XD enablement session, my LSDev UI/UX Tech Lead, Janeil Vino tasked me to create a Marketing page of any product I'd like to choose. I decided to market an phone app product so in addition to the marketing page, I needed to create a few featured pages on my mobile app design. I also did not have any opportunity to practice my Carbon Design skills so although the project was open for me to be creative, I decided to try Carbon for a more creative lay-out.

The mockup also lead a fellow LSDev VD to ask me how to create Carbon charts and I happily sent him references and was able to help him out with his upskilling. This mockup was created on an earlier version of XD with no toggle features.
